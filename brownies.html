Brownies

Description

Topped with homemade frosting, these homemade brownies are fudgy, gooey, super chocolaty, and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Frosting

  • 3 tbsp softened butter
  • 3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confectioners' (icing) sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan. After removing from heat, stir in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla in a bowl. Beat in the cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder after. Spread batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until the top is dry and the edges start to pull away from the sides of the pan. This is after about 25-30 minutes. Let it cool briefly before frosting.
  4. To make the frosting, you combine the softened butter, icing sugar, cocoa powder, honey, and vanilla in a bowl. Stir until smooth. Frost the brownies and you are done!
